i About this report

This is a historical review of completed and invoiced work. It looks at what caused our longest proposal turnarounds on jobs we've already billed, broken down by number of trips required, job priority, type of work, location, and the assigned coordinator. The purpose is to identify the patterns that have historically caused delays so we can address them in our current and future workflow — not to track open jobs in real time.

how to use Upload a new month's invoiced work spreadsheet anytime and every section refreshes automatically. The Executive Brief tab is print-ready for monthly reviews. Use the findings to inform policy decisions and to set expectations on jobs currently in progress.
